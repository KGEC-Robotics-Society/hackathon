CRITERIA

Team Size :-  1 - 3
Who Can Participate :-  Anyone
About The Hackathon :-

The annual robotics hackathon is conducted by the KGEC Robotics Society. In this hackathon, there will mainly be three domains, namely, Electronics and IoT, Machine Learning, and Mechatronics and Robotics. This hackathon will contain very beginner-friendly topics so that everyone with different levels of experience can easily participate in it. However, performance will be measured in terms of the different aspects of the competition, including teamwork and coordination, quality of the solution.

Rules :-

-> Should be familiar with atleast one of the Domains/Techstack: mentioned above

-> Each member in the team should be from the same institution

-> Each team member must contribute

-> Code base and simulation or video feedback must be provided with proper description

-> palgarsm will result in immediate disqualification

-> You can select maximum of two domains
